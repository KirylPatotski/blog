I googled myself

You suffer more in imagination than in real life, so I decided to google myself. As expected, you will find my 2 YouTube channels, individual videos, and my apps. There are also some of “my” companies, or companies with names, coincidentally, creating software. Nothing unexpected so to speak, but there was one thing that shocked me. It was the 1100 results, most of which were fake.

There is, originally a conspiracy theory, theory, that states that in the last couple of years everything almost everything on the internet is generated by scripts or artificial intelligence. While in its form, the conspiracy theory is rubbish, it contains a few valuable insights.

First of all, if you watch YouTube, you will see almost no AI-generated content, which is of sufficient length and quality, but the many comments are fake, generated by bots. Second, you can't ignore, that 99.99% of the internet's content is completely invisible. Furthermore, creating bots costs almost nothing as well as fake messages.

I think this theory is true, try it yourself, at your own risk. Otherwise, why would so many “people” suddenly write about me?
