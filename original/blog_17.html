Modern media is useless

Stop watching news

Imagine turning on the television on a Monday evening to watch the news. The news starts with some fancy animations and music and is full of cuts, and dramatic stories, reduced to one minute and it hits you. You realize, that there was not a single thought, inside of the news - ever. News is entertainment disguised as information, there has never been a moment, where the news changed the course of your life or at least your day, instead, news, just like any media, simply distracts and entertains. Never has watching the news provided you with valuable information, so stop it, and you won't miss it. Instead, focus and what's valuable.
