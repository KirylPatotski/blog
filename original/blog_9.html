Sometimes you need to fight new technologies

VR and AR are a thread

VR stands for Virtual Reality, while AR stands for Augmented Reality, one an alternative to the real world and the other one a perfect integration to the real world. This essay will focus mainly on AR, as this technology is much more potent.

This essay is created on the occasion of the invention of VR 55 years ago, my own experience with AR, and a leading company's announcement of their own AR headset.

Before this essay gets downhill, here are the benefits of AR and VR:

Higher engagement, comfort, intuitive experiences…

Now, let's analyze the true problems of these new technologies. First of all, what problem does AR solve? Every business needs a problem to solve, for example, my apps solve the problem of calculator dependency caused by lowered mental math capabilities. The bigger the solved problem, the bigger the reward for your business. Bill Gates made computers available to the mainstream and became the richest person in the world. Visualize what kind of problems that solved. Slow communication, inconvenience, limited reach… These solved problems were gigantic and in accordance, his reward. Now, what problem does AR solve? The most obvious answer is that AR enables you to get rid of heavy hardware and provides a single solution for your personal computer, phone, or tablet - all in one. In that sense, it may be worth it. What problems does AR ACTUALLY solve? If you try looking it up on the internet, you will find some useless “use cases” like better visuals, applications in instructions, or better explanations. But these are not very big problems to solve, or not even desirable.

Now you may be tempted to comment, that phones also don't solve any problems themselves. Radios exist, torches exist, cameras exist, telephones exist, and computers exist. The only thing that differs is the size and availability. This problem is fixed by an ordinary mobile phone. So, what problem does AR solve, that phones don't?

More potent stimulation!

I could end the essay right here, but let me explain in the next 1 000 words why more stimulation is going to end everything.

Have you ever tried to read a book that was written more than 100 years ago or have you ever tried to watch a movie from 1960? Perhaps. As you noticed, it's decently hard to do that if you spend any minute of your life on the internet. You will find the movies from back then too boring to watch because they are lower resolution, and have fewer cuts, less music, less effect, and less stimulation. Because of the internet, especially social media, you will find that extremely hard.

The internet started with websites, with text, some style, and nothing else. It had no animations, no colors, nothing. If the internet would have stopped here, we would have had all the benefits: search, email, digital libraries and it should have stopped right here. Then came a few websites that includes images and later videos. Decades later, even stimulating videos became too boring and in 2016 TikTok was born. TikTok is used as a representation here because other social media is not much better. But like any social media, it is completely destructive in its very nature - because of its stimulation.

Well, why is stimulation bad? Because stimulation paralyzes. It makes you unable to think properly, unable to just sit in the room and stare at the clock for as little as 15 minutes without any thoughts. It makes it impossible to be productive in any way. I am writing this essay in one sitting and I don't have a clock in my room, only sometimes I listen to music in the background. Because I never used things like TikTok, I find that doable. But I know that this is different for most people. If you have read so far into this text and did not look anywhere else, you have a decent attention span, contrary to most people, having an average screen time of 6 hours a day on their phone. I don't want to comment on how much of your youth, of your best years gets lost and how much talent, passion and experience some people sacrifice. 6 hours of screen time compounds. 6 hours is the equivalent of 50% of your waking hours! The time you spend on your phone is not only lost forever, like any other time you spend, it is wasted in a destructive manner. Instead of doing something for your health like sleeping, most people are, extremely is an understatement, sleep deprived. Instead of doing some sports, most people are laying in bed with a spine like a question mark. I think you understand why phone usage itself is bad and gets even worse if it gets more engaging, more stimulating, and more addictive

Now, let's use this knowledge and transfer it to more stimulating AR, which is even more available than your phone because instead of it being in your pockets it's on your face. What is going to happen if you are no longer limited to turning off your phone, but need to use AR to live, because otherwise, you will feel stimulation deprived? Your life is going to get a lot worse if you start using AR instead of your phone because it has a new magnitude.

Does anyone sincerely think that phones made their life better?

Did your phone benefit in social situations, relationships, productivity, and navigation? No, most people don't even know how to use a map and compass anymore, without your phone, you are more productive and less distracted, and your “calendar-productivity-app” doesn't add any productivity, and phones made it significantly harder to socialize because people are just not as stimulating.

These problems are going to be magnified with AR.

Another problem of high stimulation is distraction, which REMOVES THE ABILITY TO THINK. What can you expect of the future if most people are going to lose the ability to think? Why should you take the effort to think, if you can distract yourself with some videos, if you try to think, you won't be able to because your stimulation baseline will be on another level. The future does not look bright if most people become pawns, too lazy and too stupid to think, all because of their own weakness amplified by AR, or does it?

AR is going to amplify every problem of current society; it's going to rob you of your life and your ability to think. It's also going to remove any desire to change anything because of constant distractions

Some people, mostly significant people or old people, don't use the internet at all, for one, because it's optional if you are important or old, but another reason to never use the internet is because of data collection. NOW PUT ON THAT STUPID AR-HEADSET ON YOUR FACE AND YOU WILL BECOME TRANSPARENT. Become transparent, lazy, stupid, distracted, unhappy, and lonely and the perfect foundation for a dark future is laid.

Final thoughts

If you felt scared while reading this, good, let this text be a warning. The only way to gain control is to get informed about reality. Not every aspect of reality is great, especially not with technology past 1990. But information is the only key to change, so pay attention. The only way to slow down these changes is to inform and resist these changes in every way possible. Don't ever allow anyone in your surroundings to use AR. If you are a landlord, forbid AR on your property. If you have some level of influence, inform others before it's too late!
