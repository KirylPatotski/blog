Nihilism is fundamentally flawed

You can read about the history and what Nihilism actually is on the web, I won't explain it further. Note, Nihilism itself can't be attacked, because it is a way to see the world, but its views can be attacked and potentially deemed useless. You also can't attack Nihilistic philosophers for “propagating” Nihilism, because you must never constrain thoughts.

Western philosophy, in my opinion, used to be excellent. Take Socrates, Plato, Aristotle, or Kant for example. Each one has contributed to their respective field and the content of their work laid the foundation for science, human rights, equality, thinking, and democracy, for example. They answered or tried to answer, questions on how to live properly. But Nihilism is different, because it throws everything into the trash can, by doubting the meaning of each one of these changes. Nihilism is the belief in Nothing and is therefore against religion. Nihilism dares to say, that life is meaningless.

Well, there are several massive flaws with this way of thinking.

First of all, if life is meaningless, what do we need philosophy and Nihilism for? It's illogical to do something meaningless, so why does Nihilism exist then? Let me give you another example. If a virus kills its host, the virus is purely destructive. Since the virus is purely destructive and kills its host, the virus strips away its own foundation and dies itself. Nihilism is a virus so to speak, because it removes its own foundation by stating that everything is meaningless. Therefore, Nihilism itself is meaningless, therefore Nihilism removes itself.

The second thing I have noticed is that Nihilism is used by a lot of young people as their way of life. Luckily, no person doubts that this way of life does not result in maximal happiness. But then they get trapped, because why improve, if everything is meaningless?

“In a million years you are going to be long forgotten and nothing will matter” is outright wrong in 2 nuances. First of all, in a million years the world will be a completely different one, without your existence, because every small change at the beginning creates large differences in the output. The other sense in which this statement does not show coherency represents the proper reply to such nonsense: “Well, everyone can pick a time frame where you'll be forgotten”. Nihilism is inherently against human nature because it strips away the drive for improvement and the beauty of existence itself. If nothing matters, then what about society, crimes, and morals? Why be nice and try to live the best life you can, if other people and you don't matter?

Philosophy is actually the same thing as math, and like any science has the task, to make life easier (not necessarily better) For example, email has made communication easier and faster as well as cheaper. If philosophy does not make the life of the individual better, by uplifting, but instead strips the foundation of meaning, why do we need such philosophy for?

Nihilists feel as if they are enlightened. If they see a person happy with their life, they assume (if they are immature) that this person lives in some kind of blissful ignorant bubble, which would burst, if they found out about Nihilism. No. You can still be happy, even if you know what Nihilism is. Happiness comes from fulfillment, which is the result of meaning. If you know that Nihilism questions meaning, and you accept this fact and remain/become happy, congratulations, you are complete. But those “enlightened” young Nihilists are not useful to society if they have these thoughts, also these thoughts come attached with many more detrimental downsides. If you feel that nothing matters, that you don't matter, it is a symptom of poor mental health, which is a symptom of being a loser, especially if you know this and still refuse to take action.

There is actually a way to get rid of or to make peace with Nihilistic thoughts. Accept. Stand upright. Take your life in order. Meditate. There are bad things and things no one cares about. I guess, it's easy to complain…
