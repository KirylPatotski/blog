How to prioritize

Urgency/Importance method

The diagram

At first, you should write down every single task, that comes into your mind, even if you think that some of the tasks may be useless. It's important to write them down anyway because you shouldn't constrain your thoughts if you want real results. You can write in the shortest form possible, for example: “finish assignment” or “create YouTube video on priorities”

The diagram, after you add the task

Now, after you write your tasks down, it's time to organize them. List them and position these tasks in an importance/urgency diagram. The tasks with a low urgency and low importance have no priority, meaning that you should cut them out because you should only do tasks that have a high importance and high urgency first. Tasks, that are unimportant and unurgent have a low priority, and should not be done first. If you map the tasks, you may notice that some tasks have a low priority now, but they will shift towards a high priority, or resolve themselves. That's why you need to work on the high-priority task first because that will avoid risk. The diagonal of this chart is the priority.

Now, after you have mapped your tasks, you should take the tasks with the highest priority and formulate them in great detail, for example, “Create a YouTube video about priorities” would be formulated in: record clips, and speak through the script 5 times, record me explaining everything, create a thumbnail, create description, create a title, upload, edit, animate, cut, create a short… Every task you have listed should be so insignificant, that you almost hesitate to write it down. After you have done that, make yourself accountable by estimating the time it takes to complete these tasks and by adding deadlines. You can keep yourself accountable with these measures and therefore add certainty and pressure, which are going to make you much more productive.

Accountability and deadlines are going to create high productivity and when you have also found the highest priority tasks, you will be able to deliver amazing results - and have more time to rest.
