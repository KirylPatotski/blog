Update panel - 14.1.2024

Today I recorded 4 videos and spent the rest of the day resting.

Written in the morning

As you might have heard, the last two weeks have been very unproductive, because of some nonsense - applications for internships. It was generally a 2/10 week because I was unconscious. If you are unconscious, you procrastinate and time passes a lot faster, hence you don't create any new memories or create anything productive. In October I learned that I can just do nothing for a day or two and everything is going to be set straight again. That's what I did yesterday and I feel completely conscious again. I didn't know how valuable was to be conscious until it got removed. Now I get reminded of Kant's book “Critique of Pure Reason” because what I'm about to tell contradicts it: you can restore consciousness by (1) Doing nothing; resting, meditating, staring at the ceiling, (2) Rewiring your brain; reading, listening to classical music, praying, and (3) By being slapped in the face. I knew of things because they were logical, not because I tried them out, that's why it contradicts this book, which says that it's inefficient to just think of things to find out the truth. I did nothing yesterday, I read the day before, and being slapped in the face I was, both with what I wrote yesterday and also quite literally: on Friday the 12th of January my class and I went to Dresden to a ballet. On the way out, I ran just as usually would, but then I slipped on some curbstone covered in black ice (to express something rather ironic, I rested a bit on the paving stones, before I could walk again). So that means, I regained my consciousness and now I have a bit to do, I want to record a couple of videos and complete some school work. Let me tell you, I'm not only able to obsess about that girl, I can obsess even harder on my goals. I uploaded already 33 videos, which is 66%. If I include the scrapped ones, it's closer to 45, which is 90%!

Update panel - 13.1.2024

If what I learned about that girl yesterday should turn out to be correct, I can understand her a lot better. At least I know that my phrase “I just couldn't understand why I got rejected because I don't know who is more attractive than me” had a darker twist, she supposedly had a neglectful boyfriend and their relationship ended in parallel on his behalf. Maybe I've also been a bit too cruel in my judgments about that loser, who helped her to get over it. An apology is vital, although what is obvious remains true, I still have a deep trauma related to people like him. In the past, I've written down 3 things I would not take her, which are social media usage, loser friends and her not being a virgin (I would find it wrong to dedicate to a woman for the rest of my life if I wasn't even her first pick.) Now the first two have sort of turned irrelevant and the third one increased in relevancy. I do not know how much of it is true, because I got the information second-hand and not from her. So, no one should gain any excessive hope, especially not me who struggles to un-love that girl after many disappointments and a long time that went by.
