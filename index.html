16.5.2024

I'm in a hurry today but here's what's new.

Today I managed to mount curtains on above my window, after just five hours of trial and error. It was difficult because that was not my area of competence and I measured the maximum width to be 111 cm. The curtains were 112 cm and so they had to be mounted in an unsexy way. I was only able to mount the curtains after three glasses of sparkling wine. Yesterday I tried for two hours with my mother (competent because I didn't have an alpha father) and we failed. Today I tried on my own and after a bit of wine, I got it. So, to everyone who thought I'm only good at videos, speaking, writing, programming, photography, gym, running, cooking, ... I can now claim that I can do something real. By the way, remember that I fixed the my sister's bike where I ruined the breaks after going to a mountain three months ago.

Secondly, the last movie I watched a movie was in 2022. I didn't like movies since the age of 8 or 9. Now I get to watch a romance movie because my grandmother wants me to, assuring that 'I can watch movies below an income of 20.000€ a day', which I used as an excuse since the age of 14 to not waste my time. Whether I will watch the next second movie at genuinely 20.000€ a day or in the next days, I will find out today.

I always wanted a housewife. Today at a final meeting of some of my classmate and my teacher at my teacher's home, I realized that this would only work if I'm truly rich. So far, I have absolutely nothing to show for. With a few failed apps, only a handful of connections, a few skills I began to develop just a few short months or years ago, I'm not in the position to get rich. Why does writing that down matter? Because I made the realization to take things slower and I don't feel like staying single for the next five years instead of two. So now I'm not sure whether I want a housewife at all.

To prioritize my health, I decided not to work out too hard at the gym. Furthermore, because I suffered from bad sleep in the last month, I need to do something there.

15.5.2024

An unimagineable period - three weeks - passed since I last included the girl who rejected me now almost year ago. I wonder how she is now... Did she develop, and implement change? Is she still reading*? How much did my text inspire positive change? I hope I did not damage anyone's self-esteem. Did she find the right one, is she waiting or did she take a random beta? How is her family? Did she decide on what to become? Technically, writing this could damage my reputation, because it could make me look weak, but I believe that a bit of care should balance a lot of arrogance. Even if there's a 99% chance I will never get the answers, I hope she's well.

*My website doesn't collect any data, if you don't believe me, let some programmer confirm that. The only code it uses is to display the yellow bar and to group the blogs by color when you press the button. That was implemented in June when I got the idea for a blog and had published three texts by noon of the next day and created the website. I know, that if I collected data, it would make me unable to sleep. What I know for sure is that many kids from my school read some texts, my some of my teachers read my texts, some friends red a few text, my grandmother reads them and she red/reads - that's it. That's all I know. I know that some kids from my school read since someone told me that he wanted to become a fat loser to which I responded by doing pushups.

Today was truly wonderful. I edited 3 videos, with love. Created 2 thumbnails and descriptions. In the end, I got into a very deep flow. I enjoyed every second and got things done. I don't care that I finished less then I anticipated because I hardly wasted time today. I'm 3 thumbnails and 3 descriptions short, missed the gym, and didn't start to speak for the audiobook. It was indeed too much work. Yet, I do not care. I enjoyed the process and this gives my life a lot of meaning. Love you.
