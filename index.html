11.4.2024

Today gym and run as usual. 50 minutes for the run. I will not give up running, the girl I'm no longer friends with said that I can't waste my talent given that I trained for 5 days and ran 10 km in 40 minutes.

Today I realized something that I didn't get from my perspective. I always do things, especially if they are scary and I do that so frequently you could almost write a blog with 130 entries and 280.000 words. However, if my regained desire for that girl turns out to be not an illusion, it is certain that 'she shall come to me and admit her mistake' would be quite scary, especially for a woman. Whether it be awkward, I'd deny, I don't believe in awkwardness, I say whatever I want to and that makes people think I'm funny. Maybe I should writing where I am at which times so that everything seems like a coincidence and in the case, I can start an conversation which I am more capable of then you might expect because I have at least stories to tell.
I said yesterday that I will just ignore my heart's desire, and I stay to that, I will not take action first, have done that enough already.

Tomorrow, the physics exam will be meditation, which does not mean I will take it lightly.

I'm glad to look back on the last two weeks and see that I indeed fully returned to strength, even if it was with a delay and very slow at first. I'm stable, competent, disciplined and willing to sacrifce again. I love y'all and I love my life.

10.4.2024

Something like two weeks ago I wrote that dream of problems to wake up in pain and that I don't dream of hot girls like everyone else would. Ever since I wrote that, I started to dream of said thing. However, I didn't stop dreaming of problems and today I woke up with a few painful realizations.

- Firstly, I noticed that I got a crush on this girl back. I had lost all hope initially and started to push because of this despair (#129). Nonetheless, since April 4th, I got my crush back, because I saw a girl who looked just like her pretty exactly where I predicted it the previous day. On top of that, as I went to the gym on Monday, there was a girl who also looked a lot like her and frequently gazed in my direction.
The problem is, it doesn't serve me. I can't know for sure if it was her, whether intent existed, whether she wants me, or whether she meets my criteria. I don't know anything outside of that she used to not want me and that I'm bloody naïve. If it is indeed an illusion, I have a serious problem, as I should obsess over my goals.

- Secondly, I have not kept it a secret that I go to the gym at 14:00, then take a break at home from 15:15 - 15:30, then bike 30 minutes to the route of the race I've been training for, rest a few minutes, and run the route for approximately one hour and then bike home for 10 minutes. So, usually, I'm home by 17:30, and after eating, showering, meditation, and writing these updates it's already 19:00. That's 5 hours that I sacrifice for sports every day. The issue is, that the likelihood of my victory in this race is low. Even if I give my best and run 15 km/h, there are ~80 other people registered online for the same route, 11 of them in my age group. This intense exercise brought me back to strength and productivity, which is without question and also without question that my ideal life does include such things. However, I can't think as productively when doing sports so hard, since my muscles take all the calories, next to the time I need to sacrifice. If I skipped the running part and went to the gym only, I would save 3 hours a day and a lot of calories. Perhaps I need to sacrifice running or massively scale it down. Maybe I will make a decision today after I walk the route, but it looks like I already decided what to do.

- Thirdly, my life contains too much nonsense right now. I don't have peak performance if the physics exam, running competition, crush, driving school, and so on all take away my thinking capacity. Luckily, physics becomes irrelevant on the 12th and driving school on the 17th. But still, I need to subtract a lot of things and make my life simpler. My life must only consist of my 2020 vision, and then in the afternoon gym, and perhaps a girl. I need to regain focus after regaining discipline via sports.

13:00. Been in school right now and decided to simply ignore the first point since my senses can't be trusted.

9.4.2024

13:50. I need to become more compassionate maybe.

18:07. Before I become more compassionate, less judgmental, warmer and so on, I need to tell you something. Today I looked at the school's website and there was an article about a student who went to marketing lectures in parallel to going to school. That pisses me off. I did that last year as well and no one cared. I built a few apps, started to fix my life, started a channel, started with the gym and went to lectures of business formation and marketing - no one cares! No one cares what I do. I'm, it seems, invisible. True, everyone in my school knows my name, but that's irrelevant in this moment. I don't target this guy, because he is at least doing something with his life and can tell you that I'm proud of him. Maybe he would make a great friend. But why is it the case that I need to overcompensate so hard and others don't have to? I did a lot more in my life then going to marketing lectures and get no recognition! What bothers me even more is that he is also 16 and goes to the gym no less then I do. At least I'm not stupid and see this as source of motivation. The girl didn't want me? Good. I'm irrelevant? Good. There's competition? Good.

18:15. The run today was a lot harder then yesterday, as my muscles were sore after running, gym and biking from yesterday and gym and biking today. Yesterday I ran 45 minutes and because many people were watching I ran the route form 16:05 to 16:51, 46 minutes. That may sound as if I declined, but considering the soreness of my muscles and that I always go to the gym before and bike there, that's a very good time.

19:38. Tomorrow is rest day, and I will walk the route in reverse at the same time as usual for a reason I won't write down, and to check out how many opponents I have in addition to their speed. Furthermore, I must take some pictures for my blog.
