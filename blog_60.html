Just before the finish line

And I almost quit

After I lost focus for 2 weeks, I recorded my longest YouTube video so far, a combination of the texts "Failures Kill" and "And then it hit me". It was the very first time where I talked for more than 30 minutes and it is the video where the speech was the best in any of my videos.

For some reason, after I came home from school, I didn't start to edit this video, rather I worked on this reading tool. I procrastinated. Then I started to edit. Then “I have had enough” and tried to look up automated video editing software. Then it was already 17:22. A day wasted. I was overwhelmed by this video because of its duration and therefore tried to shortcut it and I almost quit.

But then I remembered my own texts , and I regained focus by looking at the long term rather than the short term. In 10 years, I'm 26. My YouTube channel doesn't have to grow in 1 year. This liberated me and I took on the challenge of editing this video, this time with no shortcuts and fear, instead with a vision.

In the end, this day isn't wasted - but well spent, even if just in the last few hours. Never forget where you come from and where you go.
